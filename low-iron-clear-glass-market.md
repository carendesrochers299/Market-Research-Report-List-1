<p><strong>The Low Iron Clear Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Iron Clear Glass market primarily includes two types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a textured surface typically used for architectural and decorative applications. Float glass, on the other hand, involves a process where molten glass is floated on molten tin, creating a smooth, high-clarity surface ideal for windows and displays. Both types offer excellent light transmission and are favored for their aesthetic and functional properties in various industries.</p></p>
