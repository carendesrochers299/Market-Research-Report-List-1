<p><p>The Floating LNG Power Vessel market encompasses two primary types: power barges and power ships. Power barges are floating platforms equipped with gas turbines or generators that convert LNG into electricity, often used for shore-based power supply. In contrast, power ships are maritime vessels that generate electricity from LNG and can be towed to various locations, providing flexible, on-demand energy solutions. Both types serve as efficient, scalable alternatives to traditional power generation methods, enhancing energy access in remote areas.</p></p>
