<p><p>The fire sprinkler market features several types, each designed for specific activation mechanisms. Glass bubble sprinklers contain a liquid-filled glass sphere that shatters at a set temperature, releasing water. Fusible alloy sprinklers use a metal alloy that melts at certain temperatures, activating the system. Stent type sprinklers incorporate a stent mechanism that opens upon detecting heat. Heat sink type sprinklers feature a heat-absorbing component, which triggers water discharge when a predefined temperature is reached, ensuring tailored fire suppression solutions.</p></p>
