<p><p>The sodium silicate solution market is categorized into three primary types: LSS A, LSS B, and LSS C. LSS A is typically characterized by lower silica content, making it suitable for applications like detergents and paper production. LSS B has a balanced silica-to-soda ratio, often utilized in cement and construction for improved bonding. LSS C features a higher silica content, ideal for advanced applications such as catalysis and specialized industrial processes, enhancing strength and durability in various formulations.</p></p>
