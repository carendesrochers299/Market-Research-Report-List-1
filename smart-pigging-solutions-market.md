<p><p>Smart pigging solutions are critical for pipeline maintenance and monitoring, utilizing various technologies. Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) detects defects in pipelines by measuring magnetic field variations caused by metal loss. Ultrasonic Testing (UT) employs sound waves to identify wall thickness and detect flaws. Other methods may include electromagnetic acoustic transducers or visual inspections, providing diverse approaches to pipeline integrity assessment. These technologies are essential for ensuring operational safety and compliance in various industries, including oil and gas.</p></p>
