<p><p>The Large Diameter FRPM (Fiber Reinforced Plastic Material) market encompasses various types, notably Hot Rolled Steel Pipe and Cold Drawn Pipe. Hot Rolled Steel Pipe is manufactured by rolling steel at high temperatures, leading to improved malleability and strength, ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, Cold Drawn Pipe is formed at room temperature, resulting in tighter tolerances and enhanced surface finishes, making it suitable for precision applications. Both types cater to diverse industries requiring robust and reliable piping solutions.</p></p>
