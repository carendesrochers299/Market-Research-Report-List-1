<p><p>An Ultrasonic Cleaning Machine utilizes high-frequency sound waves, typically between 20 kHz to 400 kHz, to create microscopic cavitation bubbles in a liquid cleaning solution. These bubbles implode, producing powerful scrubbing action that effectively removes dirt, grease, and contaminants from surfaces, even in hard-to-reach areas. This technology is widely used across various industries, including automotive, healthcare, jewelry, and electronics, due to its efficiency and effectiveness.</p><p>The Ultrasonic Cleaning Machine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period.
