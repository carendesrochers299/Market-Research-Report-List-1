<p><strong>The Bunker Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Residual Fuel</li><li>Distillate Fuel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bunker oil market primarily consists of two types: residual fuel and distillate fuel. Residual fuel, often referred to as Bunker C, is a heavy, viscous oil used in large marine vessels and industrial applications, known for its cost-effectiveness but higher emissions. Distillate fuel, which includes marine gas oil (MGO) and marine diesel oil (MDO), is lighter, cleaner, and more expensive, suited for smaller ships and compliance with stricter environmental regulations. Both types cater to different operational needs and regulatory standards in the shipping industry.</p></p>
