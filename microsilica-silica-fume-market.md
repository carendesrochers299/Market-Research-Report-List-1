<p><p>The microsilica market comprises three main types: densified microsilica, semi-densified microsilica, and undensified microsilica. Densified microsilica has a compact structure, enhancing storage and handling, commonly used in concrete applications for improved performance. Semi-densified microsilica strikes a balance between compressibility and flowability, suitable for various construction needs. Undensified microsilica, being more porous, is primarily employed in specialized applications requiring high reactivity, such as in cement and refractory materials, optimizing strength and durability.</p></p>
