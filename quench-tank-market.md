<p><p>The quench tank market is segmented into three primary types: oil quench tanks, water quench tanks, and air quench tanks. Oil quench tanks are utilized for cooling and hardening metal products through oil immersion, offering effective heat transfer and reduced distortion. Water quench tanks leverage water for rapid cooling, ideal for achieving specific metal properties. Air quench tanks utilize air or gas to cool materials gradually, preventing thermal shock. Each type serves distinct applications in heat treatment processes across various industries.</p></p>
