<p><p>The single door magnetic lock market features two primary installation types: surface-mounted and embedded. Surface-mounted magnetic locks are affixed to the surface of the door frame, offering easy installation and adjustment, making them ideal for retrofitting existing doors. Embedded installations, on the other hand, involve integrating the lock within the door and frame, providing a more seamless and aesthetic appearance while enhancing security. Both options cater to varying security needs and architectural preferences in access control systems.</p></p>
