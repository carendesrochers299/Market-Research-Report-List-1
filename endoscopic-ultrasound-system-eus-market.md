<p><p>The Endoscopic Ultrasound System (EUS) market comprises three primary types: endoscopy, microprocessor systems, and ultrasound probes. Endoscopy involves the use of flexible tubes with cameras for internal visualization, while microprocessor systems enhance image processing and data analysis for improved diagnostics. Ultrasound probes, integrated with endoscopes, emit sound waves to create detailed images of internal organs and tissues. Together, these components drive the EUS market by enabling minimally invasive procedures and accurate assessments in various medical settings.</p></p>
