<p><p>The paint guns market features three main types: semi-automatic, fully-automatic, and manual guns. Semi-automatic guns load and fire paintballs with each trigger pull, offering a balance between speed and control. Fully-automatic guns continuously fire paintballs as long as the trigger is held down, providing rapid-fire capability ideal for competitive play. Manual guns require the user to reload and fire each shot individually, emphasizing precision and technique, making them popular for beginners and training purposes.</p></p>
