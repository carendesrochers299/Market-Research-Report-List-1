<p><p>The standalone combustible natural gas detector market encompasses various technologies, including electrochemical, infrared, and semiconductor types. Electrochemical detectors utilize chemical reactions to identify gas presence, offering high sensitivity. Infrared detectors sense gas by measuring changes in infrared light absorption, providing reliable detection over distance. Semiconductor detectors use conductive materials that change resistance in the presence of gas, offering cost-effectiveness.
