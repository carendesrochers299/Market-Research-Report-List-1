<p><p>The bicycle hydraulic oil market is primarily segmented into two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil and offers good performance, high resistance to oxidation, and effective lubrication at lower costs. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is engineered for superior performance, providing enhanced temperature stability, reduced viscosity changes, and better protection against wear. Synthetic options are often favored for high-performance bicycles, while mineral oils are popular for everyday use, balancing cost and functionality.</p></p>
