<p><p>The Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) for firefighting utilizes various foam types to enhance fire suppression. The low-expansion foam system produces foam with minimal expansion, suitable for tackling Class A fires and providing rapid surface coverage. Medium-expansion foam systems generate a moderately expanded foam, effective for Class B fires like flammable liquids. High-expansion foam systems create a highly expanded foam that can fill large areas and provide fire protection in confined spaces, ideal for structural fires and hazardous environments.</p></p>
