<p><p>The Vehicles DPF Retrofit Market includes two primary types: Cordierite DPF and Silicon Carbide (SiC) DPF. Cordierite DPF, made from a ceramic material, offers excellent thermal stability and is cost-effective, making it a popular choice for many vehicles. In contrast, SiC DPF features a higher thermal resistance and durability, making it suitable for high-performance applications and heavy-duty vehicles. Both types aim to reduce particulate emissions and comply with stringent environmental regulations while enhancing engine efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
