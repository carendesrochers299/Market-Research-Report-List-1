<p><ul><li>Vibrating Fork</li><li>Vibrating Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vibroswitch market is categorized into two main types: vibrating fork and vibrating rod. Vibrating fork level switches utilize a tuning fork design to detect changes in liquid levels, triggering alarms or control mechanisms when the fork is submerged. In contrast, vibrating rod switches employ a similar principle but feature a rod design, making them ideal for bulk solids and granular materials. Both types are widely used across industries for reliable level monitoring and control in various applications.</p></p>
