<p><p>The automotive engine emission control system market includes components designed to reduce harmful emissions. Oxygen sensors monitor oxygen levels in exhaust gases, optimizing fuel combustion. EGR valves recirculate exhaust gases to lower nitrogen oxide emissions. Catalytic converters facilitate chemical reactions to convert pollutants into less harmful substances. Air pumps inject air into the exhaust to aid in combustion. PCV valves manage crankcase gases, preventing oil leakage. Charcoal canisters capture fuel vapors, reducing evaporative emissions, thus promoting environmental compliance.</p></p>
