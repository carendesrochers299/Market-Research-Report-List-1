<p><p>The industrial fuel storage tanks market primarily includes single-wall and double-wall fuel storage tanks. Single-wall tanks feature a single outer layer, making them cost-effective but with a higher risk of leaks. In contrast, double-wall tanks consist of an inner tank surrounded by an outer layer, providing enhanced safety and environmental protection by containing leaks. While single-wall tanks are suitable for low-risk applications, double-wall tanks cater to stricter regulations and heightened safety concerns, making them increasingly popular in various industries.</p></p>
