<p><ul><li>Insertion</li><li>Portable</li><li>Inline</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal energy flow metering solutions are categorized into three main types: insertion, portable, and inline. Insertion meters are designed to be placed within existing piping systems, providing accurate measurements without full flow interruption. Portable meters offer flexibility for temporary or mobile applications, allowing users to measure thermal energy in various locations. Inline meters are permanently installed within the flow system, delivering continuous real-time data for monitoring and management of thermal energy consumption in industrial settings.</p></p>
