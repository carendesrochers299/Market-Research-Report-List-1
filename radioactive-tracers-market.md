<p><strong>The Radioactive Tracers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PET</li><li>SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioactive tracers market primarily comprises two key types: Positron Emission Tomography (PET) and Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT). PET utilizes positron-emitting isotopes to provide detailed images of metabolic processes, making it crucial in oncology and neurology. In contrast, SPECT employs gamma-emitting isotopes, offering valuable insights into blood flow and organ function. Both modalities enhance diagnostic accuracy, with PET being more sensitive and SPECT being more accessible, thus catering to various clinical needs and market demands.</p></p>
