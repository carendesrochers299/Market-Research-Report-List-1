<p><strong>The Stress Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blind Hole Method</li><li>Ring Core Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stress gauge market includes two primary types: the Blind Hole Method and the Ring Core Method. The Blind Hole Method involves drilling a small hole to measure stress, allowing for precise data collection without significant surface alteration. Conversely, the Ring Core Method requires extracting a cylindrical core from the material, providing a comprehensive assessment of stress distribution. Both methods are essential in various industries, including construction and materials testing, for evaluating structural integrity and optimizing design strategies.</p></p>
