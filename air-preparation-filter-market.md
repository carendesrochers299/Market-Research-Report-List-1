<p><ul><li>Physical Filtering</li><li>Chemisorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air preparation filter market encompasses various types, including physical filtering and chemisorption. Physical filtering employs materials such as fibers or meshes to trap particles and contaminants from the air, ensuring cleaner airflow in industrial and commercial applications. Chemisorption, on the other hand, involves the chemical reaction between pollutants and specialized filter media, effectively removing gases and volatile organic compounds from the air. Both methods are essential for enhancing air quality and protecting equipment and personnel in various environments.</p></p>
