<p><p>The float sensor market is primarily divided into two types: top-mounted and side-mounted sensors. Top-mounted float sensors are installed on the top of tanks, utilizing floats that rise and fall with liquid levels to provide accurate measurements. Side-mounted float sensors, on the other hand, are fixed to the side of storage tanks, allowing for easy installation and maintenance while detecting float position for level monitoring. Both types are essential in various industries for effective liquid management and control.</p></p>
