<p><ul><li>Halogen Lamp</li><li>HID Lamp</li><li>LED Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Car Lamp Market** includes three main types: **Halogen Lamps**, **HID Lamps**, and **LED Lamps**. Halogen lamps are cost-effective, widely used, and provide warm light but are less energy-efficient. HID lamps offer brighter, whiter light with better efficiency but are more expensive and have a slower warm-up time. LED lamps are highly energy-efficient, durable, and provide instant illumination, making them popular for modern vehicles despite higher upfront costs. Each type caters to different consumer needs, balancing performance, cost, and energy efficiency.</p></p>
