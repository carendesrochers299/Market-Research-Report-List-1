<p><ul><li>Airborne Sensor</li><li>Structure Borne Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Boiler Tube Leak Detector market consists of two main types: Airborne Sensors and Structure Borne Sensors. Airborne Sensors detect leaks by analyzing changes in sound waves in the air caused by escaping steam or gases, making them effective for large areas. Structure Borne Sensors, on the other hand, monitor vibrations transmitted through the boiler structure, identifying the specific location and nature of the leaks. Both technologies contribute to enhanced safety, efficiency, and maintenance in boiler operations.</p></p>
