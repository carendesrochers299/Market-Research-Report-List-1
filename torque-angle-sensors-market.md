<p><p>Torque angle sensors are essential for measuring torque and the corresponding angular position in various applications. The market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors utilize physical connections to measure torque directly through mechanical linkages, ensuring precise readings but potentially suffering from wear over time. In contrast, non-contact type sensors employ magnetic or optical methods to gauge torque without physical contact, offering durability and reduced maintenance. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
