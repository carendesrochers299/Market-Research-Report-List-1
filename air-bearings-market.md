<p><strong>The Air Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aerostatic Bearings</li><li>Aerodynamic Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air bearings market is primarily divided into two types: aerostatic bearings and aerodynamic bearings. Aerostatic bearings utilize a pressurized air film to support a load, ensuring minimal friction and high precision in applications like precision machining. In contrast, aerodynamic bearings generate a load-carrying film through the motion of the shaft, relying on high-speed rotation to maintain a stable air gap. Both types enhance performance in industries requiring high-speed, low-friction solutions, such as automotive, aerospace, and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
