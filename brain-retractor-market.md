<p><p>The brain retractor market encompasses various types used in neurosurgery to gain access to different brain regions. The cerebrum retractor is designed for procedures involving the cerebral cortex, offering visibility and space while minimizing tissue damage. The cerebellar retractor is tailored for operations on the cerebellum, allowing surgeons to manipulate cerebellar structures safely. The brain stem retractor focuses on procedures involving the brain stem, providing stabilization and exposure for delicate surgeries while preserving critical adjacent nerves and tissues.</p></p>
