<p><p>Bariatric surgical procedures encompass various methods designed to aid weight loss for individuals with obesity. Gastric bypass surgery alters the digestive system to restrict nutrient absorption and reduce stomach size. Adjustable gastric banding involves placing a band around the stomach to create a smaller pouch, adjustable for individual needs. Gastric sleeve surgery removes a portion of the stomach, limiting intake. Duodenal switch surgery combines sleeve resection with intestinal bypass, promoting significant weight loss. Other procedures may include non-surgical options or innovative techniques.</p></p>
