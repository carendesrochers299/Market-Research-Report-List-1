<p><p>The Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump (EVP) market comprises three main types: the diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. Diaphragm type pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create vacuum pressure, making them efficient and compact. Leaf type pumps employ flexible leaves that oscillate to generate vacuum, offering simplicity and lightweight design. Swing piston type pumps utilize pistons that swing to create vacuum pressure, providing high performance. Each type serves diverse applications in modern vehicles, enhancing their efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
