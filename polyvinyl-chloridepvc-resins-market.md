<p><p>The Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) resins market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Suspension PVC Resins**: These resins are characterized by their high molecular weight, offering excellent mechanical properties and processing versatility, often used in pipes, fittings, and profiles. </p><p>2. **Emulsion PVC Resins**: Known for their flexibility and ease of processing, these resins are commonly utilized in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. </p><p>3. **Blended PVC Resins**: These combine different PVC types to enhance performance characteristics, catering to specialized applications like automotive and construction materials.</p></p>
