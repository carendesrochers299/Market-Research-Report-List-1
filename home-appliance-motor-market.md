<p><ul><li>Brushed DC Motor</li><li>Brushless DC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home appliance motor market primarily includes two types: Brushed DC Motors and Brushless DC Motors. Brushed DC Motors use carbon brushes to transfer electricity to the motor's rotor, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in low-cost appliances. In contrast, Brushless DC Motors eliminate brushes, resulting in higher efficiency, longevity, and reduced maintenance. They are increasingly favored in premium appliances due to their quieter operation and energy savings, aligning well with modern consumer preferences for efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
