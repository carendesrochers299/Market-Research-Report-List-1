<p><p>The laser technology market is segmented into three main types: solid lasers, liquid lasers, and gas lasers. Solid lasers utilize a solid medium, typically crystal or glass, to produce light, offering high power and efficiency, commonly used in industrial applications. Liquid lasers use a dye dissolved in a solvent, providing tunability and versatility, often found in medical and research applications. Gas lasers generate light through a gas medium, like CO2 or helium-neon, known for their reliability and effectiveness in various fields, including telecommunications and manufacturing.</p></p>
