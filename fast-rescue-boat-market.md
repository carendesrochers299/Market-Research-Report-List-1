<p><strong>The Fast Rescue Boat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inboard Engine</li><li>Outboard Engine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fast Rescue Boat market includes two main types based on engine placement: Inboard Engine and Outboard Engine. Inboard Engine boats have the motor installed inside the hull, offering better stability, lower noise, and protection from external damage, making them ideal for rough waters. Outboard Engine boats have the motor mounted externally, providing easier maintenance, higher maneuverability, and space efficiency, suitable for quick operations. Both types cater to rescue missions, with inboard engines favoring durability and outboard engines prioritizing agility and accessibility.</p></p>
