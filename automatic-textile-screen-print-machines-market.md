<p><strong>The Automatic Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic textile screen print machines mainly fall into two categories: In-line and Carousel. In-line machines streamline production by moving the fabric continuously through a series of screens, enhancing efficiency and speed for high-volume projects. In contrast, Carousel machines feature a rotating carousel that allows multiple screens to be utilized for various colors in a single setup, making them ideal for complex designs and smaller runs. Both types serve different needs based on production scale and design complexity.</p></p>
