<p><p>Spreading machines are categorized into automatic and manual markets based on their operation. Automatic spreading machines streamline the fabric spreading process by using advanced technology to ensure precise and efficient laying of materials, enhancing productivity and reducing labor costs. In contrast, manual spreading machines rely on human operators to lay out fabrics, offering greater flexibility in smaller-scale operations but potentially slower output. Each type serves different market needs, with automatic machines appealing to high-volume manufacturers and manual machines suitable for bespoke or low-volume work.</p></p>
