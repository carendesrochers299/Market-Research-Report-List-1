<p><p>The Cast Iron Valves market comprises various types, including Cast Iron Check Valves, Cast Iron Globe Valves, and Cast Iron Gate Valves. Cast Iron Check Valves prevent backflow in piping systems, ensuring unidirectional fluid movement. Cast Iron Globe Valves offer precise flow control, making them ideal for throttling applications. Cast Iron Gate Valves provide a straightforward on/off flow mechanism with minimal pressure drop, suitable for pipeline isolation. Together, these valves serve critical roles across industrial sectors, including water treatment, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
