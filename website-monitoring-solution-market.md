<p><p>Website monitoring solutions are categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing greater control and security but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted on external servers, offering scalability and ease of access without extensive IT management. They enable real-time monitoring and updates from anywhere, making them popular for businesses seeking flexibility and reduced infrastructure costs.</p></p>
