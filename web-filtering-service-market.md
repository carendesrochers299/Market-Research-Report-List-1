<p><p>Web filtering services offer various market types to enhance online security. DNS filtering blocks access to harmful websites by intercepting domain requests, while URL filtering scrutinizes specific web addresses to prevent access to inappropriate content. Keyword filtering inspects content based on predefined terms, ensuring unwanted material is restricted. File type filtering restricts the downloading or uploading of certain file types, protecting against harmful downloads. Additionally, other methods may include content categorization and real-time threat intelligence to bolster web safety.</p></p>
