<p><p>The liquid formulations drug delivery market primarily includes suspensions and solutions. Suspensions consist of solid particles dispersed in a liquid medium, allowing for sustained release and improved bioavailability of poorly soluble drugs. Solutions, on the other hand, are homogeneous mixtures where active ingredients are fully dissolved in a solvent, ensuring rapid absorption and consistent dosing. Both formulations offer unique advantages, such as ease of administration and patient compliance, catering to various therapeutic needs and enhancing overall treatment efficacy.</p></p>
