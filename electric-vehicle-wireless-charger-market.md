Magnetic Resonance Technology enables efficient power transfer over longer distances and through obstacles, offering flexibility in vehicle positioning. Inductive Charging Technology relies on electromagnetic fields for close-range power transfer, requiring precise alignment between the charger and vehicle. Both technologies aim to eliminate physical connectors, enhancing convenience and reducing wear. Magnetic Resonance is ideal for dynamic charging, while Inductive Charging suits stationary applications, driving diverse market adoption.</p></p>
