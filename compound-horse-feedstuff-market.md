<p><p>The compound horse feedstuff market includes pellets, crumbles/cubes, and powder/mash, each catering to specific equine dietary needs. Pellets are compact, nutrient-dense, and easy to store, ideal for consistent feeding. Crumbles or cubes offer a textured alternative, promoting slower consumption and aiding digestion. Powder or mash, when mixed with water, forms a soft, hydrating meal, beneficial for older horses or those with dental issues. These formats ensure balanced nutrition, convenience, and adaptability to individual horse requirements, driving their demand in the equine feed industry.</p></p>
