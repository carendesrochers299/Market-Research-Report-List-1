<p><p>The furniture casters market primarily consists of two main types: swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters feature a rotating wheel assembly, allowing for easy maneuverability and directional changes, making them ideal for office chairs and mobile furniture. Rigid casters, on the other hand, have fixed wheels that only move in a straight line, providing stability and support, commonly used in heavy furniture like cabinets and workstations. Both types are essential for enhancing the functionality and mobility of furniture.</p></p>
