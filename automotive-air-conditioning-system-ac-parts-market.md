<p><strong>The Automotive Air Conditioning System (A/C) Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Orifice Tube System Type A/C</li><li>Expansion Valve System Type A/C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air conditioning system market is primarily divided into two types: Orifice Tube System and Expansion Valve System. The Orifice Tube System uses a simple fixed orifice to regulate refrigerant flow, making it cost-effective and easier to maintain. In contrast, the Expansion Valve System employs a thermostatic expansion valve to precisely control the refrigerant pressure and flow based on cabin temperature, providing better efficiency and comfort. Both types cater to diverse vehicle designs and consumer preferences.</p></p>
