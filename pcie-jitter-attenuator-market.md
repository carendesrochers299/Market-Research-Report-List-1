<p><p>The PCIe Jitter Attenuator market is categorized into two main types: Single-Channel and Multi-Channel. Single-Channel jitter attenuators are designed to process one signal channel, ensuring high-quality data transmission with minimal interference, making them ideal for applications with limited bandwidth requirements. In contrast, Multi-Channel jitter attenuators can handle multiple signal paths simultaneously, catering to more complex systems needing enhanced data integrity across various channels. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing performance in high-speed PCIe applications.</p></p>
