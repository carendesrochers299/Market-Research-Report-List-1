<p><p>High voltage cables in the electric vehicle (EV) market are essential for transmitting power from the battery to the drivetrain. These cables come in two main types: shielded and unshielded. Shielded cables have an additional protective layer that minimizes electromagnetic interference, ensuring reliable performance in noisy environments. Unshielded cables, while typically lighter and more flexible, may be more susceptible to interference. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the EV's electrical system and application environment.</p></p>
