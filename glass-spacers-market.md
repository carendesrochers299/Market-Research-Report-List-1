<p><p>The glass spacers market comprises several types, each serving specific insulation needs. Warm edge spacers enhance thermal performance by reducing heat transfer, while cold edge spacers, often metallic, offer cost-effective solutions but with less efficiency. TPS spacers combine thermal performance with structural integrity. Flexible spacers adapt to various glazing configurations, providing versatility, whereas stiff spacers ensure rigidity and structural support. Together, these spacer types enable diverse applications in energy-efficient window systems, enhancing overall performance and comfort.</p></p>
