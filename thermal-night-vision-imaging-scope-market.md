<p><strong>The Thermal Night Vision Imaging Scope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Un-Cooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenically Cooled Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal night vision imaging scope market is primarily divided into two types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging uses microbolometer sensors that operate at ambient temperatures, making them lighter, more cost-effective, and suitable for various applications. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging employs specialized cooling systems to enhance sensitivity and resolution, enabling superior performance in detecting distant or subtle heat signatures. Each type serves distinct needs across military, security, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
