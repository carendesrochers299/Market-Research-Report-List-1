<p><strong>The Medical Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric market is categorized into four main types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. </p><p>Drylaid involves carding fibers to create a web that is then bonded through mechanical, thermal, or chemical processes. Airlaid uses air to disperse fibers, creating a soft, absorbent material ideal for hygiene products. Wetlaid combines fibers and water to form sheets, commonly used in filtration and absorbent applications. Spunlaid, on the other hand, produces continuous filaments directly from melted polymers, offering durability and strength for medical hygiene products.</p></p>
