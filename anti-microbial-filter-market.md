<p><p>The anti-microbial filter market encompasses various types, including non-woven fiber filters, nano fiber filters, and porous polymeric filters. Non-woven fiber filters utilize a web-like structure to trap particles while providing antimicrobial properties, making them suitable for air and liquid filtration. Nano fiber filters leverage ultra-fine fibers to achieve high filtration efficiency and bacterial resistance. Porous polymeric filters feature a network of microscopic pores that effectively block contaminants while allowing fluid flow, enhancing hygiene in diverse applications.</p></p>
