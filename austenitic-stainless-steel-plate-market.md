<p><p>Austenitic stainless steel plates are categorized into various grades, each with distinct properties. Grade 301 is known for its high strength and moderate corrosion resistance. Grade 302 offers improved corrosion resistance compared to 301. Grade 303 is designed for enhanced machinability. Grade 304 is the most widely used, known for excellent corrosion resistance and weldability. Grade 309 excels in high-temperature applications. Grade 316 provides superior corrosion resistance, especially in marine environments. Grade 321 offers enhanced stability at elevated temperatures. Other grades include specialty and custom compositions.</p></p>
