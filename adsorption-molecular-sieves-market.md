<p><p>Adsorption molecular sieves are categorized primarily into A Type and X Type based on their structure and application. A Type molecular sieves, featuring smaller pore sizes, effectively adsorb larger molecules, making them suitable for drying and purification processes, particularly in the gas and petrochemical industries. X Type molecular sieves have larger pore openings, allowing them to adsorb various molecules, including larger organic and inorganic compounds. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing separation efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
