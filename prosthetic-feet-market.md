<p><ul><li>Mechanical Foot</li><li>Microprocessor Foot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prosthetic foot market is divided into two primary types: mechanical feet and microprocessor feet. Mechanical feet are passive devices that rely on springs and joints to mimic natural foot movement, offering durability and simplicity, often at a lower cost. In contrast, microprocessor feet utilize advanced technology with sensors and actuators to adapt to various terrains and activities, providing enhanced functionality and a more natural gait. The choice between these options depends on the user's lifestyle, mobility needs, and budget.</p></p>
