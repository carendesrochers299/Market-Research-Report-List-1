<p><p>The Liquid Leakage Sensor Amplifiers market comprises two main types of leak detection: invasive and non-invasive. Invasive leak detection involves installing sensors directly within pipes or systems to monitor fluid levels, offering precise and immediate responses to leaks. Non-invasive leak detection utilizes external sensors that detect changes in environmental conditions, such as temperature or humidity, to identify leaks without disturbing the infrastructure. Both methods aim to enhance safety and prevent damage by providing timely leak alerts in various applications.</p></p>
