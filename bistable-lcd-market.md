<p><strong>The Bistable LCD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>COG Chip</li><li>COF Chip</li><li>COB Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bistable LCD market features three main types of display technology: COG (Chip-on-Glass), COF (Chip-on-Film), and COB (Chip-on-Board). COG integrates the driver IC directly onto the glass, providing a compact and efficient design ideal for small devices. COF uses a flexible film to connect the driver IC, allowing for thinner displays suited for flexible applications. COB embeds the driver IC onto a PCB, enhancing reliability and heat management, often used in larger screens for various industrial applications.</p></p>
