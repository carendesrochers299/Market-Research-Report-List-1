<p><p>Software-Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN) is a technology that enables businesses to manage their WAN connections more efficiently, enhancing application performance and reducing costs. The market comprises two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based SD-WAN solutions leverage the cloud to provide flexibility, scalability, and ease of deployment, making them ideal for organizations with remote operations. On-premise SD-WAN, conversely, involves deploying hardware on-site, offering greater control and security while necessitating more significant upfront investment and management resources.</p></p>
