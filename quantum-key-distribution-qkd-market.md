<p><p>Quantum Key Distribution (QKD) is an advanced cryptographic technology that leverages the principles of quantum mechanics to secure communication channels. It enables two parties to generate a shared, secret random key that can be used for encrypting and decrypting messages while ensuring the security of the key itself. Unlike traditional key distribution methods, QKD provides a high level of security, as any attempt to eavesdrop on the key distribution process can be detected, allowing communicating parties to take necessary precautions.</p><p>The Quantum Key Distribution market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 21.50% during the forecast period. The integration of QKD with existing telecommunications infrastructure and developments in quantum networks are also contributing factors.
