<p><p>The space heating boilers market is primarily divided into non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, expelling excess heat through flue gases, making them less energy-efficient. In contrast, condensing boilers utilize advanced technology to capture and reuse heat from exhaust gases, achieving higher efficiency and reduced emissions. This distinction drives market demand, with condensing boilers gaining popularity due to their environmental benefits and cost savings on energy, while non-condensing models remain prevalent in some traditional applications.</p></p>
