<p><ul><li>QSFP</li><li>SFP</li><li>CXP</li><li>Cx4</li><li>CFP</li><li>CDFP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Attach Cable (DAC) market includes various connector types designed for high-speed data transmission. QSFP (Quad Small Form-factor Pluggable) supports multiple ports, typically used in data centers for 40Gbps or 100Gbps connections. SFP (Small Form-factor Pluggable) is versatile for lower speeds up to 1Gbps. CXP offers high bandwidth solutions, while Cx4 provides support for 10Gbps applications. CFP (C Form-factor Pluggable) and CDFP (C-Dimension Form-factor Pluggable) cater to higher speeds, focusing on 100Gbps connectivity, making them essential in modern networking.</p></p>
