<p><ul><li>Servo Motor Controller</li><li>Stepper Motor Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motor controllers market consists primarily of servo motor controllers and stepper motor controllers. Servo motor controllers are designed to regulate the position, speed, and torque of servo motors, offering high precision and efficiency, making them ideal for applications like robotics and CNC machinery. In contrast, stepper motor controllers manage stepper motors, which move in discrete steps, providing excellent control for applications requiring less precision, such as 3D printers and automated systems. Both types are integral to modern automation and robotics.</p></p>
