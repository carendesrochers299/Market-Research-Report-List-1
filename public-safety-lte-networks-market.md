<p><p>Public Safety LTE networks are categorized into three main types: Private LTE, Commercial LTE, and Hybrid LTE. Private LTE networks are dedicated systems designed for exclusive use by public safety agencies, offering enhanced security and reliability. Commercial LTE networks leverage existing telecommunications infrastructure for broader access but may not meet all public safety requirements. Hybrid LTE combines elements of both, utilizing commercial networks for coverage while retaining critical capabilities for emergency responders, thus ensuring resilient communication during crises.</p></p>
