<p><ul><li>SSL VPN</li><li>IPSec VPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote-Access VPN market is primarily segmented into SSL VPN and IPSec VPN. SSL VPNs utilize the SSL protocol to secure connections through standard web browsers, making them ideal for modern applications and remote work environments. In contrast, IPSec VPNs provide a more robust solution through encryption and tunneling at the IP layer, suitable for site-to-site connections and legacy systems. Both types cater to various security requirements and user preferences, driving demand across different sectors in the market.</p></p>
